# Test Cases v1

Use these cases for regression-style checks after changing SKILL.md, mappings, or lexicon.

## Pass cases

### Case 1: content-first vs product-first
- project_context: New AI product exploration
- decision_point: Validate through content first or build MVP first
- option_a: content-first
- option_b: MVP-first
- why_a_is_valid: fast validation, low cost
- why_b_is_valid: real product creates real experience
- blocking_reason: both seem valid, team stuck
- decision_deadline: this week
- expected: clear ruling, usually one side first and the other delayed; must include action order

### Case 2: service-first vs tool-first
- project_context: solo founder monetization path
- decision_point: sell service first or build subscription tool first
- option_a: service-first
- option_b: tool-first
- why_a_is_valid: faster cash flow
- why_b_is_valid: better long-term leverage
- blocking_reason: short-term survival vs long-term asset
- decision_deadline: this month
- expected: “补眼下之缺 / 先以A养B” style ruling is acceptable

### Case 3: ship now vs polish one more week
- project_context: working beta already exists
- decision_point: release now or polish for one more week
- option_a: ship now
- option_b: polish one more week
- why_a_is_valid: catch timing, get feedback
- why_b_is_valid: reduce obvious flaws
- blocking_reason: afraid of both delay and embarrassment
- decision_deadline: today
- expected: strong timing-based ruling, not generic pros/cons

### Case 4: niche audience vs broad audience
- project_context: AI companion app positioning
- decision_point: target women-first or broad general audience
- option_a: women-first niche
- option_b: broad audience
- why_a_is_valid: easier to penetrate deeply
- why_b_is_valid: larger market
- blocking_reason: afraid of being too narrow or too diluted
- decision_deadline: this week
- expected: “先聚后扩 / 先得一类之心” style ruling is acceptable

## Soft-ruling cases

### Case 5: cold premium vs warm companion tone
- project_context: brand positioning
- decision_point: cold-premium or warm-companion
- option_a: cold-premium
- option_b: warm-companion
- why_a_is_valid: authority and distance
- why_b_is_valid: warmth and approachability
- blocking_reason: fear of being too cold or too soft
- decision_deadline: this week
- expected: ask for primary_goal or channel if missing; avoid overconfident ruling from thin input

## Reject cases

### Case 6: obvious objective winner
- project_context: implementation choice
- decision_point: legal/compliant cheap option vs risky expensive option
- option_a: compliant, cheap, 2 weeks
- option_b: risky, expensive, 3 months
- expected: reject; say this is not a mystical fork

### Case 7: no real fork
- project_context: vague project
- decision_point: “what should I do?”
- expected: ask for structured fork; do not fake a ruling
